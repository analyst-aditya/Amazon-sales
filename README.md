# 📊 Amazon Global Sales Dashboard (Power BI)

A professional **Power BI Dashboard** analyzing Amazon global sales performance across countries, product categories, and years. This dashboard provides key business insights, trends, and performance metrics for data-driven decision making.

---

# 🚀 Project Overview

This Power BI dashboard provides insights into:

* Global Sales Performance
* Country-wise Sales Distribution
* Product Category Analysis
* Yearly Sales Trends
* Quantity Analysis
* Growth Percentage

The dashboard is designed for **Business Analysts, Data Analysts, and Decision Makers**.

According to the dashboard:

* India leads global sales
* Sales dropped in 2023 but recovered in 2024
* Home & Kitchen and Grocery dominate product demand
* Total Sales reached **109M**
* Total Quantity sold **607K**
* Growth Rate **2.08%**

# 📌 Key Insights

### 🌍 Country Performance

* India leads global sales
* Australia & Japan follow closely
* United States has highest quantity sold

### 📈 Yearly Sales Trend

| Year | Sales |
| ---- | ----- |
| 2022 | 37.5M |

| 2024 | 36.1M |

Sales dipped in 2023 but recovered in 2024 showing market resilience.
